Social Media Metrics I Track

Important KPIs for measuring success

Engagement Rate

Measures audience interaction with content

(Likes + Comments + Shares) ÷ Followers × 100

Reach

Number of unique users who saw the content

Unique viewers per post

Impressions

Total times content was displayed

Total views (including repeats)

CTR

Click-through rate from content to action

Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100

ROI

Business return from marketing efforts

(Revenue - Cost) ÷ Cost × 100

ROAS

Revenue from paid campaigns

Revenue ÷ Ad Spend
